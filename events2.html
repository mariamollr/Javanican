    • Javanican Music Events

    Christopher Sluka - singer, songwriter, musician and performer - purchased the Javanican Coffee House in 1994, with a vision to elevate the quality of music, art and coffee in San Diego. With the help of his many friends and celebrities, such as Sean Lennon, David Bowie, and others, Javanican become the ideal intimate showcase of inspired and talented musicians. Both the singer Jewel and Jason Mraz performed at Javanican before they became famous.

    The traditon persist and music events are held at Javanican every weekend where up rising musicians play their original music.
